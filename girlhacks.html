ArtSense

ArtSense is an interactive web application designed to help users explore, understand, and engage with artworks. Users can upload images of paintings or other visual art, and the app analyzes the piece to provide detailed explanations about its colors, style, symbolism, and possible interpretations. ArtSense also prioritizes accessibility, offering descriptive insights for visually impaired users so they can experience art in a meaningful way.
